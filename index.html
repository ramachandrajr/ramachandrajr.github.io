About

Hi there I am Ramachandra Junior. I am a Web Developer. I live at Bangalore. I work on MEAN stack most of the time. A bit of PHP, C, Python every now and then. And how can i forget my first love PERL, the first programming language I have ever learnt. I also worked on wordpress Until few days ago, but then I hit MEAN Stack. I have a love for web developement.

A couple of years ago if you would have said I'd a Web developer I'd have said "You gotta be kidding me right??". That curiosity when I first started searching for 'How websites are made on Google' still drives me a head. That feeling when I completed first Web project, I felt like I was Awesooommmeee! Just filling my journey with everything I love and Web developement is a big piece of that. Fast forward 2 years, well you have looked at my projects on the top eh, code talks enough about me.
